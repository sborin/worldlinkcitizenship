Canada

Overview Document

Canada

Startup Visa Program

Advantages
  • 1- 5 years’ pilot program commenced on April 1st, 2013.
  • 2- Economic prosperity and growing job market.
  • 3- Safe living environment, highest standard of living.
  • 4- Multicultural society that promotes freedom of expression, equal rights and respect for diversity.
  • 5- High quality subsidized education.
  • 6- Advanced health and welfare systems.
  • 7- Opportunity to sponsor your family relatives who want to immigrate to Canada.
  • 8- Immediate permanent residence for successful applicants.
  • 9- Right to apply for full Canadian citizenship after 3 years of permanent residence.

Requirements
  • 1- An intention to settle in Canada with the goal of establishing a business there.
  • 2- Have acquired training and occupational skills.
  • 3- The applicant and family members must be in good health and have no criminal record.
  • 4- The applicant must demonstrate that they are innovative immigrant investors who will create jobs and spur economic growth.
  • 5- Must meet strict eligibility requirements in terms of 3rd party commitment to the venture, Language skills, education and funds.
  • 6- Applicant and dependents must pass a medical examination.

Eligibility
To be eligible to receive a startup visa for a business venture the applicant must meet some key requirements:
  Obtain a commitment from designated entities:
  • 1- Business incubator confirming that the applicant’s business is currently participating in or has been accepted into its business incubator program, or.
  • 2- Angel investor group confirming that it is investing at least CAD 75,000 in the applicant’s business.
  • 3- Venture Capital Fund confirming that it is investing at least CAD 200,000 in the applicant’s business.
♦ The applicant must hold at least 10% of the shares in the business and no other person or entity may hold more than 50% of the shares.
♦ The applicant must meet a minimum of level 5 in the Canadian Language Benchmark Test in English or French, in speaking, reading, listening, and writing.
♦ The applicant must have successfully completed at least 1 year of post-secondary education.
♦ The applicant must show that he/she has enough funds to support himself/herself and any dependents after their arrival in Canada.

Process
  • 1- Appointment of an authorized representative to submit the application.
  • 2- Obtain commitment from angel investor, venture capital fund or business incubator.
  • 3- Submission of the application form, supporting documents and fees.
  • 4- Provide proof of language proficiency and other eligibility criteria.
  • 5- Typical processing time is within 6-8 months.
  • 6-Successful applicants will immediately receive permanent residence visa.

Cyprus

Overview Document

Cyprus

Citizenship by Investment Program

Advantages
  • 1- A European Union (EU) member country.
  • 2- The applicant can live, travel, work and study anywhere in all 28 EU countries.
  • 3- Spouse, and any dependent children up to 28 years old in full-time university education can be included.
  • 4- Safe living environment, a beautiful location with a great quality of life.
  • 5- Allows visa-free to travel to more than 150 countries, including the EU, Canada, Hong Kong, Singapore, New Zealand, etc.
  • 6- Reasonable cost of living.
  • 7- Multicultural society.
  • 8- Free access to public education and healthcare system.

Requirements
  • 1- No residency requirement.
  • 2- No language, education or age requirements.
  • 3- The applicant must prove a clean criminal record.
  • 4- The applicant must purchase and retain a residential property in Cyprus of a value at least €500,000.
  • 5- The residential property must be treated as a permanent residence and retained to maintain citizenship.

Investment Options
♦ An investment of at least €2.5 million in a single residential property used as a permanent residence; or
♦ An investment of at least €2.5 million in one or multiple investment properties, plus a minimum of €500,000 in a property used as a permanent residence.
♦ The investment properties may be rented out to provide an income for the investor
♦ To maintain citizenship, the applicant must:
  • 1- Keep the investment properties for a minimum period of 3 years.
  • 2- Always retain a real estate property with a minimum declared value of €500,000 (excluding VAT) as a permanent residence.

Process (approximately 3 months)
  • 1- Once the main applicant obtains the citizenship, he/she has to visit Cyprus in order to take an Oath.
  • 2- Subsequently, the dependent relatives (spouse and children up to 28 years old) can apply for citizenship.
  • 3- The property investment of €2.5 million may be sold after 3 years.

Cyprus

Permanent Residence by Investment Program

Advantages
  • 1- A European Union (EU) member country.
  • 2- One of the easiest countries to obtain residence.
  • 3- Spouse, and any dependent children up to 18 years old can be included.
  • 4- Safe living environment, a beautiful location with a great quality of life.
  • 5- Permanent Residents can travel freely throughout all 26 Schengen countries in Europe.
  • 6- Can apply for citizenship after 5 years of permanent residence.
  • 7- Can own companies in Cyprus and receive dividends.
  • 8- Opportunity to own Freehold property.
  • 9- No inheritance or wealth taxes.
  • 10- Low annual property taxes.

Requirements
  • 1- Must purchase a qualifying property in Cyprus.
  • 2- Must have an overseas annual income of at least €30,000 plus €5,000 per year for each dependent.
  • 3- Must deposit at least €30,000 in a Cyprus bank.
  • 4- May not be an employee in Cyprus.
  • 5- No criminal record.
  • 6- In good health.
  • 7- Must have acceptable Medical Insurance.

Investment Options
  • 1- An investment of at least €300,000 +VAT in a single residential property used as a permanent residence; or
  • 2- Two residential properties providing the total value exceeds €300,000 +VAT; or
  • 3- One residential property, plus a shop unit not exceeding 100 square meters providing the total value exceeds €300,000 +VAT; or
  • 4- One residential property, plus an office unit not exceeding 250 square meters providing the total value exceeds €300,000 +VAT
  • 5- The investment may be made personally or through a wholly owned company, or through a company owned jointly with the spouse of the applicant.

Process
  • 1- Application submitted to the Civil Registry and Migration Department of Cyprus.
  • 2- Submission of supporting documents plus evidence of intended investment.
  • 3- Decision made within 2 months of completed application.
Dominica

Overview Document

Dominica

Investor Program

Advantages
  • 1- Citizenship and a second passport for life for the applicant and dependent family members.
  • 2- Travel visa-free to more than 115 countries.
  • 3- Visa Free access to Schengen Area countries granted in May 2015.
  • 4- Enjoy tax free status.
  • 5- No requirement to reside in Dominica.
  • 6- No management or educational requirements.
  • 7- No country restrictions (Open to all applicants)

Requirements
  • 1- Applicants can make a non-refundable donation to the government fund or invest in a government approved real-estate project.
  • 2- Be over 18 years old.
  • 3- Have no criminal record.
  • 4- Provide all the documents are required in English.
  • 5- Provide a letter of application for economic citizenship addressed to the Minister responsible for Citizenship.
  • 6- Make a deposit in a bank account at the National Commercial Bank of Dominica.
  • 7- Have basic knowledge of the English language.
  • 7- Must use a government authorized agent.

Citizenship by investment program
1. The Government Fund option (non-refundable) Minimum to be invested:
  • 1- USD 100,000 for a single applicant.
  • 2- USD 175,000 for applicant accompanied by a spouse.
  • 3- USD 175,000 for applicant accompanied by up to two children under 18 years old.
  • 4- USD 200,000 for applicant accompanied by a spouse and two children under 18 years old.
  • 5- Add USD 50,000 for each additional dependent of the main applicant other than a spouse.

2. The Real Estate option (saleable after 3 years) Purchase authorized real estate with a minimum value of USD 200,000, which must be held for at least three years In addition to the cost of the real-estate the following additional government fees apply:
  • 1- Main applicant: USD 50,000
  • 2- Spouse: USD 25,000
  • 3- Dependent under 18: USD 20,000
  • 4- Dependent aged 18-25: USD 50,000

Process (between 3-4 months)
  • 1- Prepare all the documents required and submit them via an authorized agent, and pay due diligence fees.
  • 2- After approval, every applicant must sign an Oath of allegiance in front of a Notary Public, Justice of Peace or Commissioner of Oaths.
  • 3- Obtain the passport after the citizenship confirmation.
St. Kitt and Nevis

Overview Document

St. Kitt and Nevis

Citizenship by Investment Program

Advantages
  • 1- Children under 25 years old can be included.
  • 2- Inclusion of the applicant’s parents above 65 years old.
  • 3- No physical residency requirement.
  • 4- No requirement to travel to St. Kitts & Nevis during the application process.
  • 5- No interview, education or management experience required.
  • 6- Allows visa-free travel to around 130 countries, including Schengen Zone (26 European countries), UK, Hong Kong, Singapore, etc.
  • 7- No tax on worldwide income.

Requirements
The candidate must:
  • 1- Have no criminal record.
  • 2- Be in excellent health.
  • 3- Have high personal net worth.
  • 4- Make a monetary contribution to the Sugar Industry Diversification Foundation (SIDF), or an investment in a government-approved real estate development.

Process
  • 1- Application takes an average of 4-6 months.
  • 2- Completion of application forms.
  • 3- Provision of certificate of no criminal convictions.
  • 4- Medical examination.
  • 5- The process is simple, and only basic information is required from the applicant.

Investment Options

Sugar Industry Diversification Foundation (SIDF) Contribution:
This monetary contribution has various prescribed levels:
  • 1- USD 250,000–Single applicant
  • 2- USD 300,000–Family with up to 3 dependents
  • 3- USD 350,000–Family with up to 5 dependents (spouse+4 dependents under 18 years of age)
  • 4- USD 50,000–Additional per person above 7 dependents
  • 5- USD 450,00–Family with up to 7 dependents (spouse+6 dependent under 18 years of age)
  • 6- USD 50,000–For each dependent aged 18-24 years old
Real Estate Property Investment:
  • 1- The property purchased must have a minimum value of USD 400,000 in a government-approved real estate development
  • 2- The property must be owned and maintained for a minimum of 5 years
  • 3- Property registration, processing fees and taxes must be paid in addition to the property purchase.
Other Fees:
  • 1- USD 7,500 – Due diligence fees for the main applicant.
  • 2- USD 4,000 – Due diligence fees for each dependent above 16 years of age,
  • 3- 5% Stamp duty is payable on the value of the Real- estate purchased.
  • 3- For Real Estate investments, separate government fees will apply to the applicant, spouse and dependents.
