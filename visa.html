Cambodia Visa Application

I. VISA

All foreign nationals must obtain a visa to enter Cambodia except for those from countries with bilateral visa exception agreements with Cambodia. Citizens of Laos PDR, Malaysia, Vietnam can remain in Cambodia for 30 days without a visa. Citizens of the Philippines can remain here for 21 days, while Thai and Indonesian nationals can remain for 14 days. It is critical to note, however, that all foreign nationals must be carrying a passport that is valid for at least six months before they enter Cambodia. There are three key ways to obtain a visa to enter Cambodia:

1. Apply at a Cambodian embassy in another country : When doing this you will need to provide your original passport and it must be valid for at least 6 months after your entry date into Cambodia. You will also need to provide 2 photographs. The visa will be issued after 3 working days. It is a single entry visa that allows you to remain in Cambodia for one month. The visa fee is 20 US dollars for a tourist visa and 25 US dollars for a business visa.

2. Visa on arrival : Foreign nationals can obtain a 30-day tourist or business visa on arrival at the airport or any international border check points (citizens of some countries are excluded).

3. Cambodian e-Visa

E-Visa Make Your Trip Happen To The Kingdom Of Wonder!

The Ministry of Foreign Affairs and International Cooperation has launched an e-Visa service to allow you to apply for a Cambodia tourist visa online. All you have to do is fill in the online application form and pay with your credit card. You will receive your visa by email. Simply print it out and present it to the immigration officer when you enter Cambodia.

Entry Type: Single entry only
Validity: 3 months (starting from the date of issue)
Length of Stay: 30 days (more)
Processing Time: 3 working days
Requirement: A passport that is valid for more than six months from time of entry, a recent passport-size photo in digital format (JPEG or PNG format), a valid credit card (Visa/MasterCard).

Countries excluded: Afghanistan, Algeria, Saudi Arabia, Bangladesh, Iran, Iraq, Pakistan, Sri Lanka, Sudan, Nigeria (Please apply your Visa from your nearest embassy or on arrival at all major checkpoint).

II. CAMBODIA VISA EXTENSION

Tourist (T) and Business (E) visas can be extended within Cambodia at the Immigration Department or through a travel agency. (T) can be extended once for up to one month while (E) can extend from one month to three months (single entry) and six months to one year (multiple entries).
