Cambodia Visa
Application

All foreign nationals must obtain a visa to enter Cambodia except for those from countries with bilateral visa exception agreements with Cambodia. Citizens of Laos PDR, Malaysia, Vietnam can remain in Cambodia for 30 days without a visa. Citizens of the Philippines can remain here for 21 days, while Thai and Indonesian nationals can remain for 14 days.
